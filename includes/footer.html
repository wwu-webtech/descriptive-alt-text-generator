    • City of Bellingham Land Acknowledgment

      • “The Sehome Hill Arboretum Board of Governors acknowledges that Sehome Hill is traditional, ancestral and unceded territory of the Lhaq'temish – Lummi people – and the Nuxwsá7aq – Nooksack people – who have lived along the Salish Sea and throughout the San Juan Islands and the North Cascades watershed from time immemorial. We honor our shared responsibilities to care for the Arboretum, and are grateful for the opportunity to learn from Indigenous wisdom as we work to create relationships sustaining the Arboretum’s forest for past, present and future generations.”
